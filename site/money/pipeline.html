Brand Outreach Pipeline

Every pitch tracked: cold → replied → negotiating → signed → shipped → paid. Replaces a spreadsheet. Cross-device synced via data.json.

The pipeline rules

  1. 10 cold pitches/week minimum once you hit Month 3. Most will ghost. That's normal.
  2. Follow up once at Day 7, once at Day 14. If nothing → mark dead, move on.
  3. Never undercharge. Starting rate after 5K followers: $300/TT, $400/IG Reel. Up 25% for usage rights, 50% for exclusivity.
  4. Track the dead pitches. Patterns emerge — which industries respond, which don't.
  5. Inbound > outbound is the goal by Month 6. Pitch hard early to seed inbound later.

Where to find brands

  • Aspire · Mavrck · Whalar — apply to brand campaigns directly
  • Insense · Influee · JoinBrands — UGC-focused, easier to land first deals
  • Cold pitch nerd brands directly: Loot Crate–type, ThinkGeek-equivalents, indie comic shops, Star Trek merch makers, prop companies, glitter / makeup brands tied to the aesthetic
  • Inbound: media kit linked in TT bio + IG bio + email signature
