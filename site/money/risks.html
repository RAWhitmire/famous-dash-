Risks + Tradeoffs

Things that derail the plan + what to do about them.

Don't quit on one good month

$2,400 for ONE month is not the signal. 2 consecutive months at $2,400+ minimum, ideally 3. Holding the print shop one extra month costs you $2,600. Quitting too early can cost the entire path.

Healthcare runway = secret weapon

Most creators can't quit because health insurance costs $400–600/mo solo. You have 22 months on mom's plan. Use the runway aggressively. Even $2,200/mo (slightly below replace) makes you net-equivalent or better than the print shop.

Brand deal income is lumpy

You'll book 0 deals one month and 4 the next. Smooth this with TT Shop + UGC + creator funds (predictable). Weight projections: 50% predictable, 50% lumpy.

Self-employment costs eat ~25%

$2,400 gross ≠ $2,400 take-home. Self-employment tax (15.3%) + income tax (~10%) = ~25% off the top. Real take-home: ~$1,800. To match print shop $2,600 net, need ~$3,470 gross.

Algorithm risk is real

One platform shift can erase 50% of monthly views. The 9-stream stack is your insurance. Don't let any single stream cross 50% of monthly income — when it does, allocate hours to the next-largest.

DT IP enforcement could escalate

Push-strategy means takedowns will happen. Acceptable: 1–2 designs/quarter pulled. Not acceptable: account strikes accumulating. 3+ DMCA strikes in a year on one platform = pull back to original-IP-only. Tier B catalog is your insurance.

Burnout is the silent killer

Sprint cadence (3+ posts/day) is unsustainable past 60–90 days. Post-sprint plan: 2/day @rawhitmire + 3–4/wk DT + 1 long-form/mo. Protect Sunday off. UGC capped at 4/mo.

The acting long arc still wins

Every monetization stream is fuel for the long arc, not the goal. Don't optimize so hard for income that you stop pitching for self-tapes / agent meetings / acting work. Audience = leverage. Auditions = use.

Discount Trek IS the moat — push it

DIY-Trek + glitter + "fully unhinged" combo is genuinely uncommon. Generic creators get crushed on rate; specific creators get bidding wars. Push the cult-classic angle harder, not less. The IP risk is the price of the moat — it's worth it.

Forewarned = forearmed. Read this when something starts wobbling.
