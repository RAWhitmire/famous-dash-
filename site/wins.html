Wins.

The receipts. Not for the algorithm — for the long arc. Reverse-chronological, dry, specific. Reread when the algorithm is silent and the day-job feels permanent.

The wins log is for receipts you'd otherwise forget. Some of these are big. Most of them are small. Both count.

April 30, 2026

Sprint launched.

Day 1 of the 31-day sprint. M01 ships today. Whatever the next thirty days return, today is the day the publishing schedule stopped being theoretical.

April 30, 2026

The site stopped being cluttered.

Stripped the site down to reference + life. Dropped the scripts and the content calendar from public view. Editorial typography, single column, real footer. The version that doesn't make me wince when I open it on a desktop.

April 29, 2026

121 scripts, across the whole sprint.

Days 1 through 31, every main video and every Discount Trek post — written, validated, scrubbed for voice and for the work-talk rule, ready to film. The PDFs live offline. The hard part isn't the writing now; it's the showing up.

(For context: a normal creator might have a week of scripts banked. You have a month.)

April 29, 2026

The voice locked.

Got the scripts to the place where the voice reads as you, not as a TED talk. "I did not audition for that role" became the load-bearing phrase. The brand stopped sounding like a self-help book.

April 26, 2026

The Polina note arrived.

Public, in front of 67 other sprint participants. "Your hook is generic." Three minutes to either fold or use it as the spine. Used it as the spine. The note now runs through the entire sprint as the operating principle, not the wound.

April 8, 2026

The project got named.

"Becoming Famous" stopped being a thing happening in the head and started being a thing on a wall. The 21-day pre-sprint clock starts here. The 18 months before this don't disappear — they were pre-production.

August 2025 (~8 months ago)

Came back to acting.

The actress thing, picked up again at 23 after a few years of not pursuing it. Didn't announce it at the time. Just started doing the work. The sprint is the visible result of this decision.
