Operating principles the arc enforces
Principle 1 — specificity is the moat "Generic" = "could have come from anyone." If a line could come from any creator in your lane, it's not yours. Anchor every hook in something that only you would say.
Principle 2 — the public note is the gift Private feedback is suggestion. Public feedback in front of your peer group is leverage — you have to act, and the audience gets to watch you act. That's the brand.
Principle 3 — the note becomes the OS, not the wound The arc isn't "I cried about this for 30 days." It's "I rebuilt the work to honor the note, and you're watching me do it." The wound becomes the spec.
Principle 4 — never make Polina the villain She did her job. The note is correct. The brand respects the source. Anything that frames her as cruel = wrong frame.
