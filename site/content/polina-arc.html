Originals were weak — flat, narrated, no emotional variety, no rehooks. This rebuild ships through the full pipeline: trend research → diagnosis → strategy → ideation → hooks → scripts → QC. 6 new videos that replace M04 / M14 / M27 / M55 / M77 / M92.
01

Trend Research · 2026

What the algorithm is rewarding right now — direct from current creator-economy reporting.

  • 70% completion rate is the new viral threshold (up from 50% in 2024). Sub-30s videos need 50%+ avg watch time minimum.
  • Steepest drop-off = first 3 seconds. The hook is everything. Visual motion + curiosity gap + pattern interrupt all firing simultaneously.
  • Pattern interrupts every 3–5 seconds — text overlays, cuts, camera angle shifts, B-roll inserts. Static talking heads die.
  • Mid-video rehooks at 15s and 30s reset attention. Without them, drop-off accelerates after 12s.
  • Last 2 seconds should drive rewatch — loop ending, cliffhanger, or pattern that makes people watch again to "get" it.
  • Watch time + saves > likes for algorithmic distribution. Saves are the strongest single signal.
  • 2026 audience preference: unfiltered, vulnerable, BTS over polished/curated. Realism beats romanticism.
  • Best-performing arc: problem → journey → solution in <30s. Micro-narratives crush macro-narratives.
  • "Unexpected Response" trend (Charli XCX Boom Clap) is hot — text flip from setup to a surprising reply. Universal-feeling micro-moments outperform big-stakes content.
02

Diagnosis · Why the Originals Were Weak

10 problems across M04 / M14 / M27 / M55 / M77 / M92

  1. Telling, not showing. "I'm still applying the note" instead of seeing the application.
  2. No emotional arc per video. They're flat. No problem→journey→solution within each one.
  3. No rehook at 15s/30s. ~30s of monotone narration = drop-off after second 12.
  4. Captions described, didn't tease. "Three before/after hooks" isn't visceral.
  5. Polina is undefined. Viewers don't know who she is, what's at stake, why her opinion matters.
  6. No pattern interrupts. Same shot type, same energy, same vibe across the arc.
  7. The "heart react" visual is buried. Should be the hook — was the mid-video moment.
  8. Context-dependent. Viewer needed to have seen M04 to understand M14, M27, M55. Each video should stand alone.
  9. Rational instead of emotional. No fear, no rage, no triumph, no exhaustion. Just "applying notes" repeatedly.
  10. Repetitive structure. "X days ago Polina said something. I'm still applying it." 6 times in a row.
03

Strategic Framing · The Rebuild

Six core principles the rebuilt arc follows — every video must satisfy all six.

Principle 1 · Episodic but standaloneEach video plays cleanly to a first-time viewer. No "remember when Polina..." — assume they don't. Open with stakes that reset.
Principle 2 · Emotionally variedEach video has its own emotional center: shock, fear, growth, vindication, setback, vulnerability, gratitude. No two videos hit the same note.
Principle 3 · Show, don't narrateVisible artifacts in every video — phone screens, analytics, before/after, drafts. The "applying" must be physical, not described.
Principle 4 · Polina as characterShe's a phone screen, but she has weight. Her message in a specific font/timestamp. Her heart-react animating. She's a presence, not a name.
Principle 5 · Format-variedSix different formats across six videos: F04 Storytime, F03 Trend Remix, F04 Storytime, F11 Tutorial, F04+F09 Sketch hybrid, F01 Direct Address. Different shapes = different audiences.
Principle 6 · Rehook structure baked inEvery video has hook (0-3s) + rehook 1 (5-15s) + rehook 2 (15-25s) + climax (25-40s) + loop hook (final 2s). 70% completion is the gate.
