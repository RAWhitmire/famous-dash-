Polina arc · principles (unchanged)

Principle 1 · StandaloneM04 plays cleanly to a first-time viewer. Open with stakes that reset. Don't assume they've seen anything else.
Principle 2 · Real artifactsThe phone screen, the group chat, the "your hook is generic" message, the 67 competitors watching — all real. No staged reactions. No invented numbers Raquel didn't actually receive.
Principle 3 · Polina as characterShe's a phone screen, but she has weight. Her message in a specific font/timestamp. A presence, not a cartoon villain.
Principle 4 · Friend-in-a-room voiceSpecific weird details, not abstract diagnostics. "I had three minutes to reply" not "the dimming reflex activated." Concrete moments, not psychology.
Principle 5 · No work refsThe day job stays internal-only. The Polina arc is about a creative event — not a workplace event. Even M27 (originally print-shop framed) is now reframed around what was real about the noticing.
Principle 6 · Already past the struggleRaquel narrates from authority — she figured it out, she's looking back. Not modeling beginner anxiety in real time.
