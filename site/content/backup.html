Backup Content Bank

Pre-filmed videos ready to deploy on bad days, sick days, or when an idea bombs and you need a replacement fast. The insurance policy.

⚠ Why this exists

Zero-tolerance posting means you cannot skip a slot. But life will try to make you skip — sick day, family emergency, mental health crash, equipment failure, wifi outage, an idea that absolutely won't work.

The Backup Bank is your safety net. Filmed in advance, edited in advance, ready to ship in 60 seconds.

Target backup state

Minimum

3 videos

Bare minimum. Covers one bad day. Refill within 48 hrs of using.

Healthy

5–7 videos

Standard goal. Covers 2–3 bad days. Sustainable to maintain.

Comfortable

10+ videos

Sick week recovery covered. Quit-print-shop confidence builder.

What goes in the bank

✅ Good backup candidates

  • Evergreen content — not tied to a specific date or trend
  • Direct address takes — easy to film 3 in 30 min, edit individually
  • Process / time-lapse — captured naturally, narration added later
  • DT character pieces — set up cardboard once, film 4 monologues
  • Carousels — photos taken anytime, captions whenever

🚫 Bad backup candidates

  • Trend remixes — sound expires; backup goes stale fast
  • News reactions — newsjacks need same-day relevance
  • Reply-to-comment — context-dependent on recent post
  • Sprint-narrative posts — "Day X retrospective" only works that day
  • Holiday/event-specific — Cannes posts for May 10 only

Refill protocol

When you USE a backup, refill within 48 hours. Otherwise the bank silently empties and you discover it's gone on the next bad day.

  • Wednesday batch days: always include 2–3 backup-bank-quality videos in the shoot
  • Sunday filming block (3:30–5 PM): refill the bank if it's below 5
  • "No idea" days: instead of forcing a fresh post, film 2–3 backups for the bank — same effort, more leverage

5 backup videos = 5 nights you sleep instead of panic-filming.
