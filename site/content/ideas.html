Idea Bank

Quick-capture for hooks, premises, and concepts. The well of "what to film when I have time but no idea." Cross-device synced — tell me an idea in chat, I commit it.

How to use this

  1. When inspiration hits at the print shop or in the shower — don't trust your memory. Tell me in chat: "Idea: [the thing]". I commit to data.json.
  2. When you're stuck on a "no idea" day, this is page #1 to open. Pick something. Film it.
  3. Categorized by format type so you can grab one matching today's slot type.
  4. Once filmed, mark it used — moves to archive. Track which ideas became which posts.
