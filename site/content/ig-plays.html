Instagram Plays

IG-specific tactics. Currently 209 followers, low engagement. Strategy: cross-post Reels for now, invest more once TT is hitting.

The IG strategy in one paragraph

IG is a SECONDARY platform during the sprint. Primary effort goes to TT. IG gets cross-posts of your TT Reels with native captions + hashtags. Don't over-invest in IG-native content until TT is producing 1K+/week followers — that's when IG cross-traffic becomes meaningful.

During sprint (M01–M93)

Cross-post protocol

  • Same day, 30 min after TT post. Don't simul-post — IG penalizes obvious cross-posts.
  • Reupload natively via IG Reels, don't share link.
  • Strip TT watermark if present (use snaptik or similar before reupload).
  • Native caption — different from TT, written for IG audience (slightly more polished).
  • 3–5 hashtags only — IG capped engagement on 30-tag posts.

IG hashtag stack (rotate)

  • Primary niche: #soloMaker · #DIYfilmmaking · #parodycontent
  • Trek tier: #StarTrekFan · #StarTrek60 · #TNGfan · #Trekkie
  • Comedy tier: #IndieComedy · #AbsurdComedy · #parody
  • Discovery tier: #creatorlife · #behindthescenes · #soloartist

Pick 3–5 that match the post. Rotate so feed doesn't pattern-match into spam.

Stories (low effort, high signal)

IG Stories build parasocial bond without big content lift:

  • Daily 1–3 stories — behind-the-scenes, prop builds, "filming today's Slot 1" moments
  • Polls + question stickers — get audience input on next DT skit, prop choices, drink for editing
  • Repost your Reels to Stories with "new today" overlay — drives Reel views
  • Highlights organized: "Discount Trek" · "Behind the bridge" · "Process" · "Acting"
Post-sprint IG escalation

When TT hits 5K+ and you start gaining 200+/week, IG strategy shifts:

  • Add IG-native content — carousel posts, longer captions, behind-the-scenes
  • Engage with niche IG creators — Trek fan accounts, parody comedy accounts, indie filmmakers
  • Apply for Reels Bonus program when invited (regional, can't force)
  • IG = best for brand pitches — most brands check IG even for TT-primary creators
  • Update bio + media kit link to convert IG visitors to TT followers AND brand inquiries
