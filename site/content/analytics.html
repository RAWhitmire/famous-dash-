What to watch for

✅ Healthy patterns

  • +10% week-over-week followers minimum during sprint
  • One viral (>10K) by Week 2
  • Profile visits ≈ 3–5x followers gained (curiosity → following)
  • Comments per post climbing, not just views
  • Saves trending up — strongest algorithmic signal

⚠️ Yellow flags

  • 2 weeks of flat growth — format-market fit isn't there yet, change up
  • Views climbing but follows aren't — content is interesting but not "you" enough; lean harder into personality
  • High views, low completion — hooks aren't matching content; reset hook strategy
  • Comments dropping while views stable — audience getting passive; ask explicit questions, run polls
The Sunday night log ritual

Every Sunday between 7–9 PM, take 5 min:

  1. Pull current TT / IG / YT / DT follower counts
  2. Note cumulative TT views from Analytics tab
  3. Identify your top-performing post of the week
  4. One sentence: "What worked? What didn't?"
  5. Tell me in chat: "Week N analytics: TT [X], IG [X], YT [X], DT [X], cumulative views [X], top post [name]. Notes: [obs]"

5 min/week × 31 weeks = the difference between "I think things are going well" and "here's exactly what's working."

What gets measured gets managed. Sunday is review night.
