Operating system · activation hack

Five min.

Lie to yourself. Tell yourself it's five minutes. Start. Once you start, the rest takes care of itself. The lie is the feature.

Discovered the night the litter box finally got cleaned. The trick: don't think about the litter box. Think about five minutes of scooping. Once the scoop is in your hand, you keep going. You always do. The five-minute lie is the activation energy. The actual time doesn't matter — what matters is that you started. Three buckets only: the room, the laundry, the people. Add more when you find them.

Category 1

Room reset.

The litter genre. Things that compound when ignored.

  • Scoop the litter box
  • Pick the clothes off the floor
  • Clear one surface in the room
  • Throw out one round of room trash
  • Make the bed
  • Put one stack of stuff away
Category 2

Laundry.

It's its own thing. Five minutes is one move.

  • Throw a load in the washer
  • Move the wet load to the dryer
  • Fold one basket (or just the shirts)
  • Put the folded clothes away
  • Strip the sheets
Category 3

People.

A five-minute call counts. Most do.

  • Call one family member. Five minutes.
  • Text one family member back
  • Send a "thinking of you" text to whoever popped into your head
