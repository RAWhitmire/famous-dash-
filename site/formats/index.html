The Test Method

  1. Days 1–10 (M01–M30): Cover all 15 formats, each posted ≥2x. Track views, completion %, comments, saves, shares, follows.
  2. Day 10 evening: Calculate Format Score per format. Identify top 3 winners.
  3. Days 11–21 (M31–M63): 70% top 3, 20% next 3 promising, 10% bold new tests.
  4. Days 22–31 (M64–M93): 80% confirmed winners, 10% mid-tier maintenance, 10% bold experiments.
  5. Tracking happens via tracker page — fill numbers, ranking auto-derives.
  6. The point: at 160 followers, your bottleneck is format-market fit, not effort. Stop wasting time on what doesn't hit.
