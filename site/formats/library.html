Master Format Library

15 formats · 3 tiers. Tier A (gold) = high-virality, low-cost. Tier B (teal) = medium-virality, brand depth. Tier C (purple) = low-virality but high-value (loyalty / SEO / repeat-watch).
F01

Direct Address

Face to camera, conversational. No costume. Punchy hook in first 1.5 sec. 15–45 sec.
When it worksPersonality is the product. Hot takes. Anecdotes with punchline.
Example"I just realized something nobody talks about with [thing]…"
F02

POV-Trope

"POV: you [scenario]" with you playing the audience experience. Visual + caption. 10–25 sec.
When it worksUniversal experiences. Niche subculture inside jokes. Looping endings.
Example"POV: you're a Trekkie at a normie party trying to act normal"
F03

Trend Remix

Hijack a trending sound or format. Apply YOUR twist. Original spin + recognizable framework.
When it worksSound has 100K–10M uses, peak velocity, audience matches yours.
ExampleTrending "tell me you're X without telling me" + "tell me you're a solo creator"
F04

Storytime

Spoken anecdote, captions for retention. Strong opening hook. 30–60 sec. Builds parasocial bond.
When it worksTrue stories with a twist. Customer service nightmares. Filming mishaps.
Example"Storytime: I tried to film a Star Trek parody and the cardboard fell on me"
F05

DT Character (Captain's Log)

In-character on @discounttrek. Cardboard badge minimum, optional cardboard phaser/PADD. 25–50 sec. Whiteboard "viewscreen" behind. Captain-cadence VO.
When it worksBit fully committed. Cardboard visible. Dry-not-bombastic. The kitchen IS the bridge.
Example"Captain's log. Stardate: today. The badge is, as you can see, made of cardboard." (DT-01)
F06

Voiceover B-Roll

Voiceover narration over visual content. Voice + footage recorded separately. 25–60 sec.
When it worksBehind-the-scenes process. Building something. Voice-first creators.
ExampleVoiceover explaining sprint plan over footage of cardboard bridge setup
F07

Mockumentary

Deadpan fake-doc style. Cuts between B-roll and "interview" shots. 30–60 sec.
When it worksSelf-deprecation packaged as serious. Documentary Now! / Garth Marenghi energy. The desk pan in M16 is the canonical rep.
Example"Here, in her natural habitat, the writer-actress prepares for what she calls 'a Tuesday.'" (M16)
F08

Process / Time-lapse

Time-lapse of building, drawing, prepping. 15–40 sec. Speed-up music, satisfying reveal.
When it worksVisible progression. Striking final reveal. Soothing pace.
ExampleDrawing the DT bridge in Photoshop, time-lapse + reveal at end
F09

Sketch / Mini-Scene

2+ characters (you in costume changes). Narrative beat. 30–60 sec. Higher production.
When it worksYou can clear costume changes in editing. Comedic beat works on rewatch.
ExampleCaptain Discount + Salt Vampire arguing about budget through a viewscreen
F10

Reaction / Duet

React to or duet someone else's video. Borrows their reach. 15–45 sec.
When it worksSource video in your niche, recent, getting traction. You add genuine value.
ExampleDuet a Trek take video adding the DT take.
F11

Tutorial / How-To

Explain how you did something. Steps with text overlays. 30–60 sec. Saves > views.
When it worksYou're teaching something only you can teach.
Example"How to make a cardboard ENTERPRISE bridge for under $20"
F12

Day-in-the-Life Mini-Vlog

Slice of life. Multiple cuts through your day. 30–60 sec. Higher production.
When it worksShowing the LIFE behind the content (print shop, editing, the grind).
Example"Day in the life of a print shop employee who's secretly a content creator"
F13

Q&A / Audience Question

Answer a comment or DM on camera. Reply directly with text overlay. 15–40 sec.
When it worksYou actually have audience questions. TT's reply-to-comment feature amplifies.
Example"Reply to @username asking how I have time to make all this content"
F14

Carousel / Photo Dump

Slideshow of photos with text overlays. Music underneath. 8–20 sec. TT loves these.
When it worksVisual narrative. Before/after. Behind-the-scenes stills. Low-energy day. M17's "5 lines I almost cut" is the canonical rep.
Example"Five lines I almost edited out of videos this week" — 5 slides + outro (M17)
F15

Green Screen Explainer

Chroma background, you commenting over visual. 20–45 sec.
When it worksNeed to point at / annotate something visual. News reactions.
ExampleGreen screen over latest TOS 60th announcement, you commenting
