How to use this

Daily during sprint: after each post hits 24-hr mark, log the numbers per format. Average across reps gives reliable signal.

Cross-device persistence: tell me numbers in chat ("F01 averaged 12K views, 32% completion, 14 comments..."), I commit to data.json, every device sees update.

Local edits in this page (typing in the inputs) DON'T persist on reload. They're for quick math during the day. Permanent log goes via chat.
