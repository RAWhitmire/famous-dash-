Per-video log + per-format rollups. Format Score = (Avg Views × Avg Completion%) + (Comments × 50) + (Saves × 100) + (Shares × 75) + (Follows × 200).

How to use this

Daily during sprint: after each post hits 24-hr mark, log the numbers per video in the per-video table below. The per-format rollups underneath aggregate across reps.

Cross-device persistence: tell me numbers in chat ("M01: 12K views, 32% completion, 14 comments, 8 saves..."), I commit to data.json, every device sees the update.

Local edits in this page (typing in the inputs) don't persist on reload — they're for quick math during the day. Permanent log goes via chat.
