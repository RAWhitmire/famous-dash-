Sleep Reset

Fixing the oversleep cycle. Currently 2 AM → 7-8 AM (5–6 hr). Target: 11 PM → 7 AM (8 hr).
Diagnosis

Sleep debt accumulates when you bank under 7 hr for 4+ nights. Body clock breaks. Mornings get harder, not easier — that's the oversleep loop starting.

Target

Bed 11 PM, wake 7 AM = 8 hr in bed. Wed/Sun off days: 8 AM wake = 9 hr if 11 PM bedtime.

Transition plan

Shift bedtime back by 30 min every 2 nights. Tonight 1:30 AM → next 1 AM → next 12:30 → ... → 11 PM by night 6. Don't try to fix in one night. Phase shift.

Hard rule

1 AM is the latest cap on weeknights during the sprint. The 2:15 AM edits are bonus content, not the operating system.

Wind-Down Routine (last 60 min)
  1. 10:00 PM: phone moves out of bed. Charges across the room or in another room.
  2. 10:00–10:30: wind-down. No new editing starts. Wrap any open task.
  3. 10:30–10:45: teeth, skincare, water bottle by bed.
  4. 10:45–11:00: in bed. No screens (or e-reader at low brightness).
  5. 11:00 PM: lights out.

Why this matters more than you think

Under-slept Raquel = under-creative Raquel. The format-test phase (Days 1–10 of sprint) is where you find what works. Decisions made on 5 hr of sleep are worse decisions. The point of the sleep reset is not "be a morning person" — it's "don't squander the format test on tired-brain takes."
