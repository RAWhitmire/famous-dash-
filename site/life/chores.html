Sunday Chore Wheel

4-week rotation. Daily 5-min litter scoop happens every morning regardless. Sunday rotates the deeper jobs.

Week 1

  • Laundry (always)
  • Sheets washed
  • Litterbox deep clean
  • Quick bathroom wipe-down (5 min)

Week 2

  • Laundry (always)
  • Bathroom deep clean
  • Quick litter scoop (already daily)
  • Room organization 30 min — ONE corner / surface

Week 3

  • Laundry (always)
  • Sheets washed
  • Litterbox deep clean
  • Quick bathroom wipe-down (5 min)

Week 4

  • Laundry (always)
  • Bathroom deep clean
  • Optional Tier 2 batch-cook (cook double for freeze stash)
  • Room organization 30 min

Daily 5-min minimums (every morning, no negotiation)

  • Litter scoop (5 min)
  • Dishes from yesterday into dishwasher OR rinsed in sink
  • Trash check — overflowing = take out before leaving

Below this baseline = the room compounds against you within 3 days. Keep the floor.
