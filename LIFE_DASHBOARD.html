Sleep Reset fixing the oversleep cycle

Currently sleeping 2 AM → 7-8 AM (5-6 hr). That's an oversleep-cycle setup. Target: 11 PM → 7 AM = 8 hr.
Diagnosis: sleep debt accumulates when you bank under 7 hr for 4+ nights. Body clock breaks. Mornings get harder, not easier — that's the oversleep loop starting.
diagnosis
Target: bed 11 PM, wake 7 AM = 8 hr in bed. Wed/Sun off days: 8 AM wake = 9 hr if 11 PM bedtime.
target
Transition plan: shift bedtime back by 30 min every 2 nights. Tonight 1:30 AM → next 1 AM → next 12:30 → ... → 11 PM by night 6. Don't try to fix in one night. Phase shift.
transition
Hard rule: 1 AM is the latest cap on weeknights during the sprint. The 2:15 AM edits are bonus content, not the operating system.

Acting Practice Restart gently · 1 hour weekly to start

Acting career = the long arc. Without practice the brand thesis cracks. Restart small. Build over weeks, not days.

Sun 5–6 PM · 1 hour weekly

  • One monologue work-through (15 min) — pick one, stick with it for the month
  • One sides cold-read (15 min)
  • 15 min: voice / breath / warm-up
  • 15 min: notes / what felt off / journal one line
First Sunday: just 30 min. Build to 1 hour over 3 weeks.
