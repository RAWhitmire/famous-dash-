You don't find the life that's yours. You discover it by motion. This page is the first move. Five steps, seven minutes, one Tuesday. Print it. Fill it out. Pin it where you can see it. Then take the rep on page 4.
01

The scene you're in

Where the camera is pointed in your life right now. Be specific. Use names if you need to.
02

The role you've been playing

What's the script you've been reading from? "Practical daughter." "Reliable one." "Future-X if I'm being good." Name it.
03

The first reframe

Say it once. Out loud. Then write it.
starting today — I am no longer available for that role.
04

The first rep

Not a Monday plan. A Tuesday move. The smallest possible thing that makes the new direction real this week.
05

The line you take with you

Pick one. Repeat it when the old role tries to come back.
